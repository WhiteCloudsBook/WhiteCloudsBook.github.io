White Clouds
- A Sci-Fi Novel -

By: Yoav Niran

Coming soon(ish)

In the 24th century humans learned that Earth would soon no longer be able to sustain life. A fraction of humanity was spared and set course through the vastness of the galaxy in massive spaceships to settle an earth-like planet. After a journey fraught with danger they reached their destination only to discover their new home was not habitable after all. Without the resources to venture out to space again they decided to stay and build a station that will sustain them.

Now, almost 500 years later, humanity is thriving on enormous space stations, fondly called The Clouds, orbiting the hostile planet. Thoughts of finding a terrestrial home to inhabit long abandoned.
