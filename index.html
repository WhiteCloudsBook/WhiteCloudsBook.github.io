White Clouds

By: Yoav Niran

The year is 2843. More than five centuries after a fraction of humanity left a dying Earth behind. Now, humans dwell aboard giant space stations called THE CLOUDS.

Up until a few days ago, life was good for WENDALL SYING. He was top of his class in pilot-school, had good friends and a loving and supportive mother. His dream was to become an exploration pilot like his father was. That's until his mother was found dead.

Without any time to grieve, Wendall finds himself being hunted. His only link to understanding what’s going on is a recording left by his mom. In it she reveals that she was part of a vigilance group with the sole purpose of preventing a sentient AI from rising again. When last encountered, this entity had killed tens of thousands.

Whoever is hunting Wendall is trying to resurrect the AI. It's not what Wendall can do or what he knows but what he is. His very DNA is fused with the AI's code and they'll do anything to get their hands on him. Without him they cannot bring back the entity fully and fulfill their plan to instate it as ruler over humans.  

When his pursuers kill a friend sent to help him reach safety, Wendall is left completely alone. He has to decide whether to run or join the fight. If he runs, his pursuers will no doubt hurt more of his friends. If he stays to fight and gets caught the AI would be unstoppable. According to the Vigilance, setting it loose on the Clouds could only mean one thing – Annihilation.

Either way, Wendall has no choice but to confront what he cannot outrun - The part of the AI entity that's inside him.
