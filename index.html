White Clouds

By: Yoav Niran

The year is 2843. More than five centuries after a fraction of humanity left a dying Earth behind. Now, humans dwell aboard giant space stations called THE CLOUDS.

What starts as a normal day for WENDALL SYING becomes a nightmare when his mom is found dead at home. With no time to grieve, Wendall must leave everything behind following his mother’s instructions in a recording she left him. In it she reveals that she was part of a vigilance group with the sole purpose of preventing a sentient AI from rising again. When last encountered, this entity had killed tens of thousands.

Whoever is hunting him is trying to resurrect the AI and they need Wendall not for what he can do or what he knows but what he is. His very DNA is fused with the AI's code which they must have in order to bring back the entity fully - To fulfill their plan to instate it as ruler over humanity.

When his pursuers kill a friend sent to help him reach safety, Wendall is left completely alone. He has to decide whether to run or join the fight. If he runs, his pursuers will no doubt hurt more of his friends. If he stays to fight and gets caught, the AI would be unstoppable. According to the Vigilance, setting it loose on the Clouds could only mean one thing – Annihilation.

Either way, Wendall has no choice but to confront what he cannot outrun - The part of the AI entity that's inside him.
